M2M - Shades of Purple (Released in 2000)

This album was released in the US on 7 March 2000 by Atlantic Records. It reached No. 7 in Norway, No. 89 on the US Billboard 200 and No. 1 on US Top Heatseekers chart.
The album received a positive response. And it sold over 1.5 million units worldwide and was nominated for best pop album at the 2000 Spellemannprisen awards.

The Day You Went Away: The Best of M2M

This album was released in 2003 after M2M disbanded. And the compilation includes all of their singles from their two studio albums Shades of Purple and The Big Room, acoustic versions of some other songs and popular album tracks. It also contains a Mandarin Chinese version of their song "Pretty Boy" and a Spanish version of "Everything You Do".
